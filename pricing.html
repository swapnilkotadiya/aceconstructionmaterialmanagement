Frequently asked questions, answered

Q. How can you start your subscription plan ?

You can start your subscription plan by choosing from one of the 3 options namely Basic Plan, Premium Plan & Custom Plan. Features & Pricing of these plans can be viewed in the “My Plan” section.

Q. What are your payment options ?

We accept payments via debit/ credit cards. The fees of your plan are billed in advance, are non-refundable and automatically renew on a monthly, semi-annual or annual basis as described in your plan.

Q. Are my card details stored ?

In accordance with our privacy policy, your credit card details are NOT stored in our servers . The card details you enter while signing up are moved to the payment gateway in order to process your subscription renewals for each cycle.

Q. How can you upgrade your subscription plan ?

You can do so from the ‘My Plan/Upgrade’ option in the ‘Profile’ icon at the top right corner of the platform. Once a plan is upgraded, your new plan and billing cycle starts from the date of upgradation and you will be charged immediately on a prorated amount as per the increased rate and chosen billing cycle.

Q. How to delete your account ?

You can deactivate an account. Users who have registered on the platform as Project Owners or Procurement Managers can do so.
